Who Is Marcia Brazil? Meet 90 Day Fiance Mike Youngquist!

As per the report by In Touch, 90 Day Fiance star Mike Youngquist is dating former reality TV star Marcia Brazil. It comes just after he sparked reconciliation rumors with his separated wife Natalie Mordovtseva.

Below is a wiki article on Marcia Brazil, where you can learn about her relationship with Mike, her son, her age, her job, and many more.

Meet 90 Day Fiance Mike Youngquist Girlfriend, Marcia Brazil!

Howard “Howie” Wood, who represents both Mike and Marcia, told In Touch that the two are seeing each other. Recently, the duo sparked dating rumors after Marcia shared pictures of them together on a sushi date.

The Rock of Love alum added fuel to the rumors after sharing another set of photos of them looking all loved-up to her Instagram on August 15th.

Marcia’s Instagram posts came just days after Mike and Natalie sparked rumors that they may have gotten back together. Mike and Natalie separated in March 2021 after less than a year of marriage.

They appeared to reunite in a teaser trailer for Season 3 of 90 Day: The Single Life. In the trailer, Natalie could be seen returning to Mike’s home in Sequim, Washinton, carrying flowers.

Natalie added fuel to the reconciliation rumors when she shared a throwback picture of her and Mike on August 23rd. To a fan asking “Are you back together?”, she replied by saying, “[Maybe] we never parted …”

Rumors of Mike and Marcia possibly being a thing first began to spread when Marcia shared a selfie of her featuring Mike taken at the Hilton Lake Las Vegas Resort & Spa on September 26, 2021. The next day, Marcia also shared pool selfies of her and Mike. Despite all these strong pieces of evidence, Mike always maintained that he was not seeing any woman after his split with Natalie.

Around the same time, Natalie tried to attack Mike and Marcia’s relationship by saying, “He is on vacation with his new Catch.” Marcia then took to Instagram to take a dig at Natalie by sharing, “Give the person you love honesty, respect trust & passion or be prepared to watch from the sidelines as someone else does it.”

Does Marcia Brazil Have A Son?

Marcia Brazil has a son named Austin Cash. On November 3, 2013, Marcia took to Instagram to share a picture of her newly born son. In the caption, she wrote, “Here’s baby Austin… My son.”

Marcia’s son takes a special place in Marcia’s life. On her Facebook bio, she has written “My son is everything! I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me. Love and Be Kind!”

Marcia features her son quite often on her Instagram. On July 28, 2021 post, she shared a little gaming setup she had made for her son because he wants to be a gamer. She also shared that her son will be posting videos to YouTube and Twitch and that she will be helping him to gain followers by streaming on her own after he goes to sleep.

On August 19, 2022, Marcia posted a clip of her son Austin pulling a prank on her, which she seemed to enjoy.

As of August 2022, we had no information about the baby’s father. From her Instagram, it looks like she raises Austin on her own.

Marcia Brazil Age

Born on August 8, 1979, Marcia Brazil’s age was 43 as of this writing.

Marcia Brazil Job

As a reality TV star, Marcia Brazil first starred in Rock of Love in 2009 with Bret Michaels. She later went on to appear in Charm School with Ricki Lake and I Love Money.

Besides being a TV star, Marcia also works as Sales Executive at Image 2000, according to her LinkedIn handle.

Furthermore, Marcia also has her own store on Amazon, where she puts her favorite products. She earns commissions every time a buyer goes on to buy stuff from her store.

Although we aren’t sure yet, she also owns a bakery store, The Cookie Shoppe located in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Is Marcia Brazil On Instagram?

Marcia Brazil is huge on social media. On her Instagram account (@marciabrazil), she has over 100 thousand followers. She also streams on the Twitch platform. You can find her on Facebook and YouTube as well.

Marcia Brazil Family

Much of Marcia Brazil’s family is still in Brazil. She often posts family-related stuff on her Instagram.

On August 7, 2011, Marcia posted a picture of herself, her mother, and her grandmother with the caption “Three Generations!!!!.” Marcia has featured her grandmother more than any other family member. It seems like she really loves her.

On August 22, 2020, she posted a picture with her sister. Now that we are talking about her siblings, it’s worth mentioning that she has also a brother, whose identity remains a mystery to us.

Marcia took a trip to Brazil to meet her family in 2021 and through Instagram, she shared that her trip to Brazil was an absolute dream of hers.

Marcia Brazil Plastic Surgery
