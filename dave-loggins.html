Biography

Dave Loggins was born in Tennessee on November 10, 1947. Singer-songwriter best known for his hit single ‘Please Come to Boston,’ which hit number one on the U.S. Easy Listening charts. His collaboration with singer Anne Murray earned him a CMA Award for Vocal Duo of the Year in 1985. He wrote the number one single “Morning Desire” for singer Kenny Rogers. He was born in Mountain City and worked as an insurance salesman before turning to music.
