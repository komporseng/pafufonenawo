Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom Cane Sugar location

Certain ingredients in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom can be incredibly useful for cooking and giving great stat boosts. This includes Cane Sugar. We will walk you through how to get your hands on Cane Sugar in Tears of the Kingdom, as it isn’t the easiest ingredient to find in the game.

Where to get Cane Sugar in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Instead of hunting around Hyrule to find it naturally, you can go ahead and purchase it from a couple of vendors if you have the Rupees to spare. You can purchase it for 12 Rupees at the Goron City General Store (1684, 2463, 0386). Equally, you can get Cane Sugar from the Slippery Falcon in Rito Village (-3645, 1820, 0185).
