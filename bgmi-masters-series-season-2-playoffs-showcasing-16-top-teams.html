Berlin Season 2: Will There Be a Second Season of The Netflix Series Berlin?

Berlin Season 2: Berlin is a TV show from Spain made for Netflix. It’s created by Álex Pina and Esther Martínez Lobato. This series is like a story before the main events of Money Heist, showing the life of Andrés de Fonollosa, known as Berlin, before the first two seasons of the original show.

The main actors are Pedro Alonso, Michelle Jenner, Tristán Ulloa, Begoña Vargas, and Julio Peña Fernández. Netflix released the series on December 29, 2023, and it has eight episodes. Money Heist: Berlin got mixed reviews, with some critics feeling it didn’t match the excitement of the original Money Heist.

Now, fans wonder if there will be a second season of Berlin. They’re eager to know if it will happen or not. This article will give you all our information about Berlin Season 2. Keep reading to find out more about it.

Will There Be a Second Season of The Netflix Series Berlin?

Currently, there’s no official word from Netflix or the creators about whether Berlin and his gang will be back for a second season. Usually, Netflix considers what viewers think before deciding on a sequel. Considering how well the first season did, it looks likely that we’ll get a Berlin season 2.

The first season was announced in 2021 and came out in 2023. If we follow the same timeline, we might need to wait about two more years to find out what happens next in the big heist. So, patience will be key as we look forward to the next chapter.

Who Will Be in The Berlin Season 2 Cast?

In the first season of the show, the story ended at an exciting point, and it hinted that all the characters from Berlin season 1 might return if there’s a second season. One of the main actors, Pedro Alonso, who plays Berlin, seems very likely to come back since the end of season 1 suggested that he wanted to bring his team together again for more adventures. This means fans can look forward to seeing the whole group reunited, and there’s also a chance that new characters might join in.

So, if there is a Berlin season 2, here are the actors and the roles they might play:

  • Tristán Ulloa as Damián
  • Samantha Siqueiros as Camille
  • Michelle Jenner as Keila
  • Begoña Vargas as Cameron
  • Julio Peña Fernández as Roi
  • Joel Sánchez as Bruce

These actors could form the core cast of Berlin season 2, bringing their characters back to life for more thrilling episodes.

What Can We Expect from Berlin Season 2 Story?

Berlin is a skilled thief in the TV series. In the first season, he and his gang successfully pulled off a jewel theft, which wasn’t surprising given Berlin’s reputation. Even though they faced some challenges along the way, they managed to escape without getting caught. At the end of the season, we see Berlin and his gang planning another big heist. It seems like the show might follow a pattern of having one heist per season.

As for what the next heist will be about, we can only guess. The first season also introduced some romantic storylines that could be further explored in future episodes.

In terms of relationships, Berlin’s connection with Camille seems somewhat positive but there’s an underlying distrust between them. This tension could lead to problems in the second season. Additionally, Berlin’s right-hand man, Damián, has his own relationship issues that might stir up trouble within the group due to his jealousy.

If the second season introduces new characters, which seems likely, it could create more complications, especially for the couples that formed during the first season. Overall, Berlin’s crew might face even more challenges and conflicts as they delve into new heists and navigate their personal relationships in the upcoming season.

Berlin Season 1 Finale Recap

In Episode 8 of Berlin, Roi and Cameron evade the police by cleverly escaping through tunnels, leaving behind decoys in their RV. While the police scramble to track them, Sierra discovers their ruse. Meanwhile, the gang splits up: Bruce and Keila dispose of evidence and prepare for further trouble, while Camille contemplates betraying Berlin. Roi and Cameron successfully flee to Spain, bonding over their troubled pasts. They reunite with the gang at a safe estate.

Bruce and Keila evade capture by stealing a boat and outsmarting the police. Camille, urged by Susi, decides to blackmail Berlin instead of contacting the authorities. They track him down, compelling him to comply with their demands. Sierra, Lavelle, and Murillo strategize for future encounters with the gang, expecting another heist. Berlin, conceding to Camille and Susi’s blackmail, plans another job with Damián, indicating the gang’s ongoing adventures. Despite police efforts, the gang remains one step ahead, ensuring their saga continues amidst escalating tension and uncertainty.

Where to Watch Berlin Season 2?

If there’s going to be a second season of the show Berlin, it will likely come out on Netflix. This is because the first season of Berlin is already available for streaming on Netflix. So, if they make another season, it will probably be released on the same platform.

Is There a Trailer for Berlin Season 2?

Unfortunately, there isn’t a trailer out yet for the second season of Berlin.
