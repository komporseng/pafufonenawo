The Singer Stuns In These Photos On Stage &amp; More Hollywood Life

H.E.R. (born Gabriella Wilson) is one of the most talented artists out there. We were all introduced to the moniker H.E.R. with the release of her self-titled debut album by RCA Records.  

H.E.R. stands for “Having Everything Revealed.” And man, has the Vallejo, CA native built quite the fanbase with her absolutely raw, vulnerable, contemporary R&B ballads.

Today, she has collected a whopping 82 wins and 184 nominations for her music. 

And not only does she have the singing chops, but also seems to have a pretty great sense of style. If you thought her name was a mystery, well then get ready for her wardrobe, because it adds a whole new level of mystery. 

If you’ve seen practically any photo of the singer, then you’ve definitely seen her sporting her signature pair of shades.
