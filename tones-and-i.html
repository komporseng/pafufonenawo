Career and Professional Life

Tones and I is an Australian indie-pop singer, songwriter as well as musician. Prior to her fame, she worked busking in Melbourne and also worked in a fashion retail shop, Universal Store.

Likewise, she created her YouTube channel in 2009. She started performing in local gigs and festivals, including the Let Go festival.

In 2014, she even performed as a vocalist for a duo. Later, she bought an RC300 (loop station) with the money made from her retail job.

As of August 2020, her YouTube channel, Tones And I, has already garnered over 3.95 million subscribers, with almost 1.5 billion total views.

She initially started posting Cappella cover versions in her early career. She toured Byron Bay to try busking there in September 2017.

Similarly, she met Jackson Walkden-Brown over there. Later, Jackson became one of her talent managers. However, she later quit her job and was seriously determined to pursue a career in music.

In 2017, she even won the Battle of the Buskers at Buskers by the Creek. Also, in 2018, she lived out of her van in Byron Bay.

Similarly, she signed a contract with Bad Batch Records/Sony Music Australia in February 2019. She even signed a co-management deal with Artists Only and Lemon Music.

She released her debut single, “Johnny Run Away” to an Australian website, Unearthed. Later, the single was sent to the Australian radio station Triple.

It became a huge success and she officially released the single which peaked at number 12 on the ARIA Singles Chart.

On May 10, 2019, She released her second single, “Dance Monkey“. Her second single became a huge hit and topped number one in over 30 countries.

They are Australia, France, Germany, Ireland, Japan, Malaysia, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, New Zealand, the United Kingdom, Belgium, Austria, Canada, China, Denmark, Finland, Portugal, Sweden, Switzerland, the United Kingdom, and South Africa.

It topped at number 5 in the United States.

Similarly, she broke the record for the most weeks atop the ARIA Singles Chart by any artist in November 2019. Ed Sheeran’s “Shape of You” earlier endured atop for 15 weeks.
